Lemon Pie

Best Pie

When you think of the best pie you think of lemon pie.
It's zesty and it's bold.

Tips and Tricks

  • keep the ingredients cold
  • chill the dough for a flaky crust

The recipe

ingredients quantity
crushed graham crackers 1 1/2
butter melted 6 tablespoons
Granulated sugar 1/3 cup
sweeted condensed milk 3 cup
egg yolk 3 large
freshly squeezed lemon juice 2 lemons
kosher salt 1/8 teaspoons
heavy whipping cream 2 cups
confectioners'sugar 2 tablespoons
McCormick vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

instructions

  1. Combine crushed graham cracker, melted butter, and sugar in a medium bowl. Mix well and press into greased pie dish..
  2. Bake for 8 minutes at 350°F.
  3. Carefully remove from oven.
  4. While pie crust is baking mix sweetened condensed milk, egg yolks, lemon juice and salt in a stand mixer. Turn mixer to medium and mix well for about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour into baked pie crust and bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and let rest on the counter for about 10 minutes. Place pie in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours to set.
  7. When you are ready to serve pie, mix whipped cream, confectioners sugar, and vanilla in a clean and cooled bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment. Whip until fluffy, about 3 minutes. (Can also use a hand-held mixer)
  8. Scoop onto cooled lemon pie and serve immediately. (If using lemon zest as a garnish, add before serving.).

Preperation time:1 hour preperation, 1 hour to cook

Servings: 8 servings(about 8 slices)
